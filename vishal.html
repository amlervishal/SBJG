Vishal Amler

Vishal Amler, an Architecture and urban design professional, moved to New York to pursue a master's degree in Urban Design at Pratt Institute, where he graduated with honors and received accolades such as the Outstanding Performer and Pratt Circle Award. He holds a Bachelor of Architecture degree from Aayojan School of Architecture, Jaipur, India where he graduated with distinction, and is currently registered as an Architect with the Council of Architecture India.

With a rich background in the field, Vishal brings over 12 years of professional experience from India, where he contributed to educational and residential projects and played a pivotal role in award-winning initiatives. Vishal has further honed his skills and is currently working as an Architectural Designer at SBJ Group.

As an architectural designer, Vishal is driven by soft skills in parametric & generative design, harnessing technology to create dynamic and efficient solutions. His expertise extends to streamlining workflows, aiming to enhance productivity and bring a fresh perspective to the field.

Additionally, Vishal has been honored with a prestigious fellowship at the Institute of Public Architecture, where he actively contributes to building community initiatives in and around New York City. He also contributes as a design jury member for MSUD programs at Pratt institute. In addition to his architectural credentials, he likes to work on UI/UX full-stack projects and collaborate with other developers on a regular basis. He has built front-end projects for companies like SBJ Group and GEMS Education.
