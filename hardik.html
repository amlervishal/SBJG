Hardik Gupta

Hardik Gupta joined SBJ Group in 2023. He completed his bachelors degree in Architecture in 2019 from India and worked in a design-bid build firm where he participated in all phases of design from schematic to on-site in residential, commercial projects and design competitions. The wide exposure of Architecture, Engineering and Construction coupled the interest in AEC industry, graduated with MS in construction Engineering and Management from Stevens Institute of Technology, New Jersey. During his masters he worked as a VDC project Engineer Intern in a construction management firm in New York City.
