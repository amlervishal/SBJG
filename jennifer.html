Jennifer Cheuk

Ms. Cheuk began her career with SBJ Group in 1998, shortly after graduating from Carnegie Mellon University. She became a principal of the firm in 2007.

Working closely with Stephen Jacobs for more than 20 years, Ms. Cheuk has developed an understanding of the purpose of the building should be the starting point for its design. She has focused on producing highly efficient layouts coupled with engaging designs maximizing the potential for each project, from high rise residential buildings, super talls, affordable housing, offices, logistics spaces, and more.

Ms. Cheuk has enjoyed mentoring a team of talented architects and leading complex multifaceted projects from conceptual design through construction.

Registered Architect: NCARB : New York NY 2004 | Florida 2023
