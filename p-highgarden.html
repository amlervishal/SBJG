Highgarden Tower

New Rochelle, NY
2024

Client

Georgica Green Ventures, LLC

Services

Architectural Design
Interior Design

Project Size

275,000 gsf

Highgarden Tower is a new 19-story, 275,000-sf residential building providing 219 workforce housing units. Building includes a 10,000 SF incubator space, a parking garage for 146 cars. Amenities include resident lounges, play room, exercise room, on-site supportive offices, a garden. A corner plaza, open to the public, is designed to connect the building to North Avenue and the nearby Metro North train station. As the building is located at a prominent gateway into New Rochelle, it was important for the building to stand prominently in the skyline. A limestone-colored precast concrete was selected for the cladding.
