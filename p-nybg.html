Project Size

133,000 SF

2850 Webster Avenue is a 12-floor contemporary apartment building, offering 188 thoughtfully designed residential units and a gross floor area of 133,000 square feet. The project is dedicated to providing a comfortable and affordable living experience for seniors, ensuring a community that thrives on companionship and well-being. The carefully curated amenity spaces are designed to foster community engagement and wellness. From communal lounges to fitness areas, every corner of 2850 Webster Avenue is crafted with the residents' quality of life in mind.
The building is also designed to maximize local programs and provide much-needed housing to the formerly homeless. It also meets the requirements of Enterprise Green Communities, Energy Star, and HPD's sustainability requirements that surpass the Energy Code. The terraces include a green roof and urban gardening planters for the residents. Developed under the NYC FRESH (Food Retail Expansion to Support Health) program, NYBG is committed to promoting health and well-being by providing convenient access to fresh and nutritious food options for its residents.
The building is Phase I of the redevelopment of a long-underutilized site with amazing views of the neighboring New York Botanical Gardens. The second phase, poised to start construction, is located at 2868 Webster Avenue and will provide an addition of 277 affordable apartments
