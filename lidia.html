Lidia Kim

Lidia Kim, a Senior Interior Designer and Project Manager, joined the SBJ Group in 2015, bringing with her a wealth of experience and expertise. She completed her Bachelor's degree in Interior Design at Parsons School of Design in 2005, specializing in various project types such as residential, commercial, branding, education, and hospitality.

Lidia's work is characterized by her creative mindset, deep understanding of design principles, and ability to create visually stunning and functional spaces. Her meticulous attention to detail, problem-solving skills, and knack for combining innovative ideas with technical proficiency consistently surpass client expectations. Prior to joining the SBJ Group, Lidia honed her skills at a specialized interior design firm where she focused on commercial projects, branding initiatives, hospitality venues, and high-end residences. This valuable experience exposed her to a diverse range of projects, further enhancing her capabilities as a well-rounded designer. Lidia's commitment to her craft extends beyond her professional life, as she actively seeks inspiration from various sources to stay ahead of industry trends and emerging techniques. Currently residing in New Jersey with her husband and their dog, Lidia's unwavering passion for design, adaptability, and dedication to delivering exceptional results make her an indispensable asset to the SBJ Group.
