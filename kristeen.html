Kristeen Arnold

Kristeen Arnold joined SBJ Group in 2018. Previously working in all phases of design in commercial, educational and high-end private residences. She is a graduate of Fashion Institute of Technology with a Bachelor of Arts in Interior Design 2014 and previously a graduate at NYU with Bachelor of Arts in Anthropology.

Originally from Seattle, her focus is in sustainability, universal design, as well as combining previous experiences in fashion, art and culture. Kristeen is a NCIDQ certified Interior Designer and member of ASID, IIDA and Associate AIA. She lives in Brooklyn with her husband, son and daughter.
